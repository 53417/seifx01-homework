Hamilton's Hometown

Margaret Elaine Heafield was born August 17, 1936, in Paoli, Indiana, to Kenneth Heafield and Ruth Esther Heafield (née Partington); she has a younger sister named Kathryn.

Paoli (/peɪˈoʊli/) is a small town in Paoli Township, Orange County, in the U.S. state of Indiana.The population was 3,677 at the 2010 census. The town is the county seat of Orange County. The town has ski stations, many bars and restaurantes and nice parks.
